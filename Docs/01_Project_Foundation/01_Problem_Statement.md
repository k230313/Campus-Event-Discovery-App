## Problem Statement

Campus life offers a wide range of events including academic workshops, social gatherings, club activities, and career-related sessions. However, students often miss opportunities due to fragmented communication channels, lack of centralized information, and poor visibility of upcoming events.

Information about events is typically scattered across emails, social media, posters, and internal portals, making it difficult for students to discover relevant activities aligned with their interests.

### Key Challenges

- Fragmented event information across multiple channels
- Low student awareness of available opportunities
- Lack of personalized event recommendations
- Inefficient event management processes

Many institutions still lack simple, student-friendly applications tailored to their specific needs.
